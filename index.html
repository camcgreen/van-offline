PIR Baton Lights

The passive infra red ensure lights illuminate when required and switch off when no longer needed
Ideal for illuminating large interior areas. Equivalent to fluorescent tubes, the light output is closer to daylight, creating a more comfortable working environment and improving the visibility in confined areas.

Being LED there is a low power draw, longer life and reduced vehicle downtime.
